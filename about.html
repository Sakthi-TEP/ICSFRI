About — ICSFRI 2026

First International Conference on Sustainable Futures through Responsible Innovation — organized by Team Elite Publications

Conference Overview / Introduction

The First International Conference on Sustainable Futures through Responsible Innovation (ICSFRI – 2026), organized by Team Elite Publications, is a focused international platform for students, researchers, academicians, and professionals to present and discuss ideas that advance sustainability through responsible innovation. The conference emphasizes research and practices that combine technological progress with ethical, environmental, and social responsibility.

ICSFRI 2026 aims to foster interdisciplinary exchange, support young researchers, and promote solutions that align with global sustainability goals. Selected submissions will have opportunities for publication in conference books and indexed journals.


Scope of the Conference

1
Sustainable Engineering & Green Technology
Innovations in eco-friendly engineering and low-carbon technologies.
2
Renewable Energy & Smart Systems
Design and deployment of clean energy solutions and intelligent energy management.
3
AI, Digital Transformation & Data for Sustainability
Use of AI and digital tools to accelerate sustainable outcomes.
4
Responsible Innovation & Circular Economy
Business and design models that reduce waste and maximize resource reuse.
5
Sustainable Agriculture & Environmental Management
Food systems, climate resilience, and conservation strategies.

Note: The scope is intentionally broad to encourage interdisciplinary submissions. Other related topics that advance sustainability and responsible innovation are welcome.

Who Should Attend

  • Undergraduate and Postgraduate students from all disciplines

Purpose of the Conference

The primary purpose of ICSFRI 2026 is to encourage and promote responsible innovation that advances sustainable development. We aim to:

  • A
    Promote Interdisciplinary Research
    Bring together ideas from engineering, science, social sciences, and business for integrated solutions.
  • B
    Disseminate Impactful Work
    Facilitate publication and wider dissemination of high-quality research outputs.
  • C
    Encourage Young Researchers
    Provide a supportive environment for students and early-career researchers to present and grow.
  • D
    Foster Collaboration
    Create partnerships among academia, industry, and policy for real-world impact.
