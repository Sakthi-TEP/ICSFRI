Camera-Ready Paper (CRP) Instructions

Ensure your final paper meets all formatting, citation, and submission guidelines for ICSFRI 2026 proceedings.

Important Guidelines

Before submitting the final camera-ready paper, please carefully review and follow these instructions:

  1. Ensure the plagiarism score is less than 20%.
  2. All figures and tables must have captions and be numbered appropriately.
  3. All figures and tables must be cited in the text.
  4. Verify the paper’s title, authors’ names, affiliations, spacing, and details are accurate.
  5. Provide the corresponding author’s email before the abstract.
  6. The title should accurately summarize the paper’s content.
  7. List author names and affiliations in the correct sequence without titles like Dr., Mr., Professor, etc.
  8. Do not use prefixes (Dr., Mr., etc.) for authors.
  9. Ensure the paper is well-written and thoroughly proofread.
  10. References in the paper’s body must follow the ICSFRI/IOP template.
  11. Present the methodology or approach clearly and in detail.
  12. The article should have a strong organizational structure.
  13. Include all graphs and illustrations in high-resolution.
  14. Use Equation Editor software to type all equations.
  15. Reference list must follow the correct format.
  16. Do not include author biographies at the end.
  17. Avoid first-person pronouns like I, we, us throughout the paper.
  18. Do not mention your institute, center, or co-authors in the acknowledgments.

Submit the camera-ready paper as a Microsoft Word document to icsfri2026@gmail.com. PDF-only submissions are generally not accepted unless using LaTeX and following the template.
